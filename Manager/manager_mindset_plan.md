# 30-Day Manager Mindset Understanding Plan

## Goal
Understand how managers think, what pressures they operate under, and how to navigate difficult situations by developing empathy for their role.

## Week 1 — Foundations of Management Thinking
### Read
- *The Manager’s Path* by Camille Fournier — widely recommended for engineering leaders. citeturn5search10
- *High Output Management* by Andy Grove — classic on managerial leverage. citeturn5search10

### Focus
- Understand transition from IC → manager.
- Learn what managers optimize for: alignment, delivery, communication.

## Week 2 — People, Teams, and Conflict
### Read
- *Peopleware* — importance of team cohesion and productivity. citeturn5search11
- *Five Dysfunctions of a Team* — dynamics of team conflict. citeturn5search10

### Focus
- Why managers prioritize trust, relationships, conflict resolution.
- How psychological safety ties to output.

## Week 3 — Leadership Patterns & Real Manager Behavior
### Read
- *Managing Humans* — real stories from engineering managers. citeturn5search10
- *An Elegant Puzzle* — systems of engineering management. citeturn5search12

### Focus
- How managers make decisions under ambiguity.
- How they balance business pressure, org design, and team well-being.

## Week 4 — Strategic Execution & Organizational Pressure
### Read
- *The Hard Thing About Hard Things* — executive-level problem navigation. citeturn5search10
- *The Effective Executive* — decision-making and prioritization. citeturn5search14

### Focus
- How managers think about outcomes, risk, trade-offs.
- How business constraints shape their decisions.

## Weekly Reflection Prompts
- What pressures is my manager likely under this week?
- How can I reduce cognitive load for them?
- Where can I communicate proactively?

## Practice Routines
- Summaries: send crisp updates (impact → metrics → risks → asks).
- Proactive alignment: clarify expectations before execution.
- Issue framing: present problems with options, trade-offs, recommendation.
